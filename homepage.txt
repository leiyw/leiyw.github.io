== About me
I am currently a [http://www.humboldt-foundation.org/pls/web/pub_hn_query.humboldtianer_details?p_lang=de&p_externe_id=4268809 Humboldt Research Fellow] at the Machine Learning group of University of Kaiserslautern, headed by [https://ml.informatik.uni-kl.de/ Prof. Marius Kloft]. Previously, I was a Research Assistant Professor at Southern University of Science and Technology, and a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at City University of Hong Kong. I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science at Wuhan University in 2014.

I received the B.S. and M.Sc. degrees in 
computer engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology ([http://www.iust.ac.ir IUST]), and the [http://ece.ut.ac.ir/ University of Tehran], Iran, in 2005 and 2008, respectively. In April 2015, I received the PhD degree in Telecommunications under the supervision of 
 [http://www.people.kth.se/~mikaelj/ Prof. Mikael Johansson] and  [http://www.people.kth.se/~carlofi/ Prof. Carlo Fischione] at [http:www.kth.se KTH].
